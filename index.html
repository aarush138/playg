About PLAY G°

PLAY-G˚ is a youth-driven platform built to transform the way people access and share sports equipment in their communities. Born out of the realization that many children and teenagers miss out on the joy of playing simply because they don't own the right gear, PLAY-G˚ provides a smart, local solution: rent what you need, or list what you already own. Whether it's a football left unused at home, a cricket bat sitting in the corner, or a pair of badminton rackets collecting dust — PLAY-G˚ allows users to put their idle gear to work. Through our platform, people can easily list their equipment and start earning passive income when others in their area rent it. On the flip side, anyone who wants to play but doesn't own equipment can now affordably rent it for a few hours or a day.
